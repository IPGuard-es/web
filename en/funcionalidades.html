Everything we do for you

What does IPGuard protection include?

From the moment you connect your account until it is hardened and monitored. This is everything we do so you never lose any of your social media accounts or your website.

Who has access to your accounts?

We review who can log into each account, which people still have access, and whether anyone who shouldn't have it still does (former employees, old agencies, etc.).

Account hardening

We enable two-factor authentication, verify that your recovery emails and phone numbers are correct, and close every door a hacker could use to get in.

Meta Business protection

If you use Meta Business to manage your ads, Facebook pages, or business Instagram, we review who has access, which administrators exist, and remove the ones that shouldn't be there.

Automatic backups

We automatically save your posts, photos, videos, and account data. If you lose your account, you don't lose months or years of work.

You lose nothing even if you lose your account

If your account gets blocked or stolen, you have everything saved to start again: your content, your data, your history. You don't start from zero.

24/7 security alerts

We monitor your accounts around the clock. If someone changes your password, logs in from a new device, or does anything unusual, we notify you instantly.

We detect if someone is impersonating you

We search for fake accounts using your name, logo, or photos to scam your followers or customers. If we find any, we help you report and remove them.

We teach you to spot scams and phishing

90% of hacks start with a fake message (an email, a DM, a link). We teach you and your team to recognize these tricks so you don't fall for them.

If you get hacked, we act immediately

Your password has been changed, your email swapped, or you can't access your account. Our team acts fast with a proven protocol to stop the attack and begin recovery.

We recover your stolen account

If your social media account or website has been stolen, we guide you step by step to recover it. We prepare the evidence, contact the platform, and don't stop until you have it back.

Action plan for the worst-case scenario

We prepare a clear plan so you know exactly what to do if you get hacked: who to call, what steps to follow, and how to act without wasting time or improvising.

Control who accesses what

If your team, agency, or a freelancer has access to your accounts, we organize who can do what. No unnecessary access or passwords shared over WhatsApp.

Which devices are logged in?

We check which phones and computers have active sessions on your accounts. If there's one you don't recognize, we close it and notify you.

Connected apps that could be a risk

Did you know that many apps you connected to your Instagram or TikTok still have access to your account? We review which ones they are and remove those that could be a risk.

What matters most comes first

If you have multiple accounts, we protect the one you'd be most affected by losing first: the one that generates sales, has the most followers, or the one your customers use to reach you.

Review every 14 days

Every two weeks we automatically re-check all the security of your accounts. If anything has changed or there's a new risk, we detect it and notify you.

Training for your team

We teach the people who manage your accounts how to avoid traps, use secure passwords, and spot fake messages. Because the weakest link is always people.

Evidence and documentation to file claims

If you get hacked, we save all the evidence: screenshots, access logs, detected changes. So if you need to file a claim with the platform or submit a report, you have everything organized.

We protect all your social media and your website against hacking: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, X, Twitch, Facebook, and websites. Hardening, 24/7 monitoring, backups, and stolen account recovery.
